This Cookie Policy explains how EthicAI ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our platform. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used by website owners to make their websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

  • Performance Cookies
    • Analytics and statistics
    • Error monitoring
    • Load balancing
  • Functionality Cookies
    • User preferences
    • Language settings
    • Content customization
  • Targeting Cookies
    • Usage patterns
    • Feature recommendations
    • Service improvements

4. Cookie Lifetime

  • Session Cookies (temporary)

5. How We Use Cookies

6. Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Allow only essential cookies
  • Set cookie preferences by website
  • Receive alerts about cookies
